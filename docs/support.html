Common Questions

How do I create more than one project?

Your first project is free. If you want unlimited projects, use the in-app purchase screen in NeedleNote. Purchases and restores are handled by Apple's App Store.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open NeedleNote, go to the paywall screen, and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID that made the purchase.

Where are my PDFs, notes, and row counters stored?

Your project data is stored locally in the app on your device. If your device is backed up through Apple services, that backup behavior depends on your Apple settings.

How do I report a bug?

Open a GitHub issue and include your iPhone model, iOS version, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
