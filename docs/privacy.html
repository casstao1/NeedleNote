Privacy Policy

NeedleNote is designed to keep your knitting project information on your device. We do not operate a user account system, advertising network, or analytics service for the app.

Information You Add To The App

NeedleNote lets you create and store knitting projects, row counters, row ranges, notes, photos, PDF patterns, highlights, and drawing annotations. This information is created by you and is stored locally by the app on your device.

Data Collection

NeedleNote does not collect, sell, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers or analytics providers.

If you choose to import a photo or PDF, the app uses Apple's system pickers so you can choose the file or image you want to add. NeedleNote only stores the content you select for use inside your projects.

Purchases

NeedleNote may offer an optional in-app purchase for unlimited projects. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment information, billing, refunds, and transaction processing. NeedleNote receives the purchase status needed to unlock paid features, but does not receive your credit card number.

Backups And Apple Services

App data may be included in device backups or other Apple system services depending on your device settings. Those services are controlled by Apple and your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

NeedleNote is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, visit the NeedleNote Support page.
